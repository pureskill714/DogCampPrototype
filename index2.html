About Us

Born from a love of dogs and the outdoors

Fort Canning Dog Camp started in 2019 when founder Mei Lin realised Singapore's dogs were spending too much time in lifts and too little time on grass. We set up camp on the Hill and never looked back. Today we run tailored training programmes for every breed, age, and energy level — led by certified trainers who speak fluent dog.

  • ✓
    Registered with Singapore's Association of Professional Dog Trainers
  • ✓
    Breed-specific expertise for local favourites — Shih Tzus, Goldens, Toy Poodles and more
  • ✓
    Positive reinforcement methods exclusively — no punishments, ever
  • ✓
    Progress reports sent after every session so you can continue training at home

Our Dog Camp

A place where every tail wags freely

Nestled in the lush greenery of Fort Canning Park, our sessions combine certified training with open lawns, shaded trails, and real-world stimuli — creating deeper, longer-lasting results in an environment your dog will genuinely love.
