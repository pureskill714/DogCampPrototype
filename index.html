Why Choose Us

Training your pup should feel like an adventure

We combine certified training expertise with the magic of Fort Canning's open green spaces for sessions your dog will actually look forward to.

🌿

Nature-Based Learning

Fort Canning's trails and open lawns provide real-world stimuli — wildlife sounds, varied terrain, and fresh air — that create deeper, longer-lasting training results.

🎓

Certified Trainers

All our trainers are certified and experienced with Singapore's most popular breeds. We use positive reinforcement methods exclusively — no punishments, ever.

🐾

Personalised Profiles

Every pup gets a detailed profile so trainers understand their personality, quirks, and health needs before day one. No cookie-cutter sessions here.

👥

Small Group Sizes

We cap all group sessions at 6 dogs to ensure every pup gets individual attention and feels safe in the environment — never overwhelmed.

📱

Easy Online Booking

Manage everything — profiles, bookings, session notes, and progress reports — from your phone. No calls, no fuss, just happy dogs.

🏥

Safety First

All attending dogs must be vaccinated and we carry a first-aid kit at every session. Our trainers are trained in canine emergency response.

About Us

Born from a love of dogs and the outdoors

FAQs

Questions we hear a lot

Still curious? Drop us a message and we'll get back within a few hours.

Does my dog need to be vaccinated?

Yes — all attending dogs must be up to date on their core vaccinations (DHPPiL and Rabies). We'll ask for documentation when you register your dog profile.

What age can puppies start?

Puppies can join our Foundations programme from 8 weeks old, as long as they have received their first round of vaccinations at least 7 days prior.

What happens if it rains?

We monitor the weather closely. If heavy rain or lightning is forecast, we'll reschedule and give you at least 2 hours' notice. Singapore weather means we're pros at rescheduling gracefully.

Can I stay and watch the session?

Absolutely! Owners are welcome to observe from a distance. In fact, we encourage it — the final 10 minutes of each session include a handover with tips for continuing training at home.
